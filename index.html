What Autism Is:

Autism, also known as Autism Spectrum Disorder (ASD), is a developmental condition that influences how individuals communicate, interact socially, and perceive the world around them. People with autism may have unique strengths, different ways of learning, and distinctive perspectives.

What Autism Is Not:

Autism is not a disease, mental illness, or something that needs to be cured. It does not mean a person lacks intelligence or potential. Rather, it represents a different way of thinking and experiencing life.
